Personality

Denji's traumatic past and complicated relationships, especially with Makima, leave lasting emotional scars, which he tries to cope with by seeking affection and a sense of belonging. Despite his flaws and misguided decisions, he matures into a more responsible person, showing care for those he loves, especially as he wrestles with his identity as Chainsaw Man and the internal conflict between his desires and moral choices.
